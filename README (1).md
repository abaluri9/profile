## Recent TILs (Today I Learned)

- **Kafka**: `max.poll.records` tuning dramatically affects consumer lag under high throughput
- **Spring Boot**: `@Transactional` on private methods silently does nothing — JDK proxy limitation
- **Redis**: `SCAN` vs `KEYS` in production — why `KEYS *` can block your entire Redis instance
- **Kubernetes**: Difference between `Readiness` and `Liveness` probes — got this wrong for months
- **PostgreSQL**: `EXPLAIN ANALYZE` vs `EXPLAIN` — always use ANALYZE in dev, never in prod under load

*(These come from real debugging sessions, not docs)*
